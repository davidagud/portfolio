I created this web app for a friend who was frustrated that all habit tracker apps she tried either lacked questions she wanted and/or required her to scroll daily through tons of questions she didn't care about. This app that lets you choose what questions you track, and soon will let you make your own questions.

Frontend: Angular 8, Angular Material, and Bootstrap. Backend: REST API made with Node 12 and MongoDB. Deployment: AWS S3 and Elastic Beanstalk.

In development: create your own questions, select multiple answers, autocomplete from previous answers in open-ended questions, a more robust data page, and front and backend input validation.
